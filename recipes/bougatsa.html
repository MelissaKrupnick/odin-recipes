Bougatsa Greek Custard Pie

Ingredients List

For the filling
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup semolina flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • zest from one orange
For the pastry
  • 12 phyllo pastry sheets thawed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted

Recipe Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients
  2. Mix the ingredients
  3. Plate your dolmas and enjoy

Note: this recipe is best enjoyed with good company.
